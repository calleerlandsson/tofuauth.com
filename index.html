Tofu Authenticator

Tofu is an iOS app that generates one-time passwords to help you protect your online accounts.

These passwords can be used in conjunction with your normal password when you sign into services like Google, Facebook, Dropbox, Amazon, and GitHub.

Tofu works with all services that provide two-factor authentication using the HOTP and TOTP algorithms.

It does not require a network or cellular connection and can be used in airplane mode.

Features include:

  • Adding accounts both manually and by scanning QR codes
  • Searching for accounts
  • Support for counter-based and time-based passwords with 6 or 8 digits
  • Password generation using the SHA1, SHA256, and SHA512 algorithms
